the .spr.txt format works as follows:
each number represents a hardcoded color in the parser.

0 - don't render this pixel
1 - black
2 - dark green
3 - light green
4 - dark blue
5 - light blue
6 - yellow
7 - yellow-brown-issh
8 - brown
A - white
B - gray
C - dark brown
D - unalloc
E - unalloc
F - unalloc

rows of image are demarkated by |
spaces demarkate pixels in row
